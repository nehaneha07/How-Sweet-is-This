KitKat - A Delicious Treat!

KitKat is a chocolate-covered wafer bar confection created by Rowntree's of York, United Kingdom, and is now produced globally by Nestlé, which acquired Rowntree in 1988.

Interesting Facts

  • KitKat was first created in 1935 in York, England.
  • KitKat is made with 100% sustainable cocoa from the Nestlé Cocoa Plan.
  • KitKat is the best-selling chocolate bar in the United Kingdom and Japan.

About Nestlé

Nestlé is a Swiss multinational food and drink processing conglomerate corporation headquartered in Vevey, Vaud, Switzerland. It is the largest food company in the world, measured by revenue and other metrics, since 2014. Nestlé's products include baby food, medical food, bottled water, breakfast cereals, coffee and tea, confectionery, dairy products, ice cream, frozen food, pet foods, and snacks. The company has more than 2,000 brands globally, including Gerber, Purina, Perrier, Nescafé, and Nestlé Toll House.
